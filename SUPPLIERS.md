## SOURCING STRATEGY (Phase 2)

Six-month launch timeline requires white-label or established contract manufacturer.
Local Mauritius formulation ruled out. Australian suppliers preferred for:
- "Made in Australia" premium positioning
- TGA regulatory credibility
- Reef-safe mineral (zinc oxide / titanium dioxide) chemistry commitment
- Proximity to Indian Ocean markets (DHL/FedEx express freight viable)

Target landed cost: **Rs 300 per 100ml** (1 AUD ≈ 33.89 MUR as of May 2026)
