# Tec-Tec Cream — Brand

## Brand Name
Tec-Tec Cream

## Origin Story
Named in memory of Xavier ("Tec-Tec"), Cedric's late brother, who passed from skin cancer seven years ago. A fixed donation per tube sold goes to a Mauritian skin cancer cause partner (intended: Link to Life).

## Non-Negotiables
- Xavier's story is told with dignity, never exploited.
- Donation must be real, transparent, verifiable, visible on pack.
- Reef-safe mineral chemistry (zinc oxide / titanium dioxide) is a brand commitment, not a marketing claim.
